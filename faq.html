FAQs

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

What types of stays are available?

We offer Glamping Triangles and Standard Camping Tents to suit different tastes and budgets.

How can I make a booking?

Simply click “Book on WhatsApp” on any stay page or click the chat icon at the bottom right.

Is food available on-site?

Yes! We serve evening snacks, BBQ dinners (veg & non-veg), and breakfast as per the menu on your stay page.

What should I carry with me?

Bring warm clothes for nights, personal toiletries, a water bottle, and any prescription medications.

What’s the cancellation policy?

• 7+ days before: 70% refund
• 5–6 days before: 50% refund
• Less than 5 days: No refund

Is parking available?

Yes—complimentary parking is available at the site.

Are pets allowed?

🐾 Yes! Pets are welcome. Please keep them supervised and clean up after them. Bring their bedding and food if needed.

Is Campingsthan LGBTQ+ friendly?

🏳️‍🌈 Absolutely! We welcome everyone regardless of gender or orientation. Respect and inclusivity are core to our ethos.
